We are a modern, fresh Restaurant offering Bar & Grill and so much more. From Afternoon Teas to Frank Parker Steaks and our legendary Sunday Roasts, we ensure our menus have something for everyone. The Circus Restaurant is nestled in the Warwickshire countryside with spectacular views across it's own park and lake and is based in The Heart of Stockholm Conference & Events Centre. We know how to look after people to a high and professional standard. We have circus performances at indoor dining space where you can watch the exciting performances and the delicious food together with your family and friends.
